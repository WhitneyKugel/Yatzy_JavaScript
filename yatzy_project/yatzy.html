How to Play

Each round, you roll the dice up to three times.
Save the dice you want after each roll.
After you've rolled three times, score in any category.
If you do not have the required dice for a category, you earn zero points.

Point Values

Ones: All Ones
Twos: All Twos
Threes: All Threes
Fours: All Fours
Fives: All Fives
Sixes: All Sixes
Upper Bonus: If you score 63 points or more, you earn a bonus 35 points!
3 of a Kind: Three matching dice. Score is total of all dice.
4 of a Kind: Four matching dice. Score is total of all dice.
Small Straight: Four dice in a row. 30 points
Large Straight: Five dice in a row. 40 points
Full House: Three of a Kind & one pair. 25 points
Chance: Any dice combination. Total score of all dice
Yatzy: All dice are the same. 50 points on first Yatzy, 100 points for each additional Yatzy
